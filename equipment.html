Equipment

The Group employs a range of specialised equipment selected for reliability, modularity, and effectiveness across varied operational environments.

Mobility

Mobility relies on both air and ground vehicles.

Support Equipment

Communications are provided by the discord. Breaching capabilities include hydraulic tools for controlled access. Every operator carries an individual first aid kit with dressings and bandages, plus team-level casualty evacuation equipment.

Procurement Philosophy

Equipment is selected based on operational requirement rather than cost or availability. We maintain equipment at higher states of readiness than conventional forces, with weapons zeroed and equipment packed for immediate deployment. Every operator maintains personal kit to the highest standard; equipment failure in training is treated as seriously as failure in operations.
