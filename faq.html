Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age requirement to join?

Applicants must be at least 13 years of age. There is no upper age limit provided you can meet the standards of Selection. This is because discord is 13+.

Do I need previous special forces or military RP experience?

No.

Can I transfer from another unit within BRM5?

Yes.

Is there a rank structure or is it operator-based?

We maintain a rank structure for administrative and command purposes.

What happens if I fail Selection?

Candidates who fail Selection may re-apply. We provide feedback on areas requiring improvement. Persistence and the ability to learn from failure are qualities we value. We expect to see improvement between attempts.
