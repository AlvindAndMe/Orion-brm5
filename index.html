Special Air Service

Elite special forces for Blackhawk Rescue Mission 5. Conducting counter-terrorism, special reconnaissance, and direct action operations with precision and discretion.

Our Mission

The Special Operations Group maintains a constant state of readiness to deploy at immediate notice anywhere within the BRM5 theatre. We conduct unilateral special operations in support of strategic objectives, providing the Commander with capabilities that do not exist elsewhere.

Our approach combines ruthless professionalism with tactical innovation. We operate in small teams, relying on skill, speed, and surprise rather than brute force. Every operator maintains the highest standards of physical fitness, marksmanship, and decision-making under pressure.

Core Capabilities

Direct Action

Precision raids, hostage rescue, and high-value target acquisition conducted with surgical accuracy in complex environments.

Special Reconnaissance

Covert observation, environmental preparation, and intelligence gathering deep within denied territories.

Counter-Terrorism

Rapid response to terrorist incidents, including aircraft and building intervention, IED disposal, and threat neutralisation.

Aviation Operations

Air assault deployment, tactical airlift coordination, and rotary wing insertion/extraction capabilities.

Ready to Serve?

Selection is open to all BRM5 personnel who meet the physical and mental standards. No previous special forces experience required.
