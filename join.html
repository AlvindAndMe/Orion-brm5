Join the Group

Selection is open to all BRM5 players who meet the standards. The process is demanding but fair.

Requirements

  • Minimum age: 16 years
  • Working microphone and Discord
  • English language proficiency
  • Minimum 4 hours weekly availability
  • Mental maturity and discipline
  • No active bans from BRM5

The Process

  1. Application: Submit via Discord using the template provided
  2. Interview: Voice interview with recruitment staff
  3. Selection: Four-day assessment of skills and endurance
  4. Training: Six-week foundation course
  5. Assignment: Posted to Sabre Squadron

Ready to Commit?

Click the button below to open the recruitment modal. Copy the application template and join our Discord to submit your entry.

What to Expect

Selection is designed to identify individuals who possess the mental resilience and physical stamina required for special operations. You will be tested on navigation, endurance, tactical decision-making, and teamwork. The process is intentionally stressful to simulate operational pressure. Not everyone passes, and passing does not guarantee acceptance; we assess whether you are the right fit for our culture and mission.

If you believe you meet the standard, we encourage you to apply. Whether you succeed or fail, you will learn something about your capabilities. Many candidates report that Selection, even when unsuccessful, was a valuable experience that improved their performance in other areas of BRM5.
