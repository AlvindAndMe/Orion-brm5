Our Mission
Disciplined capability, trained for complex environments.
Operations

What we do

The SAS conducts specialist operations, advanced training and unit development to maintain operational readiness across BRM5 theatres.

Principles

  • Discipline: rigorous standards in planning and execution.
  • Precision: measured, intelligence-led action.
  • Adaptability: training for diverse environments and contingencies.

Training

Training cycles are structured around mission profiles: urban, mountain, maritime, and arid environments. Emphasis is placed on live-fire, navigation, medical, and communications drills.

Ethos

Professionalism, accountability, and continuous improvement underpin all activity.

Training calendar

Quarterly cycles: Basic, Advanced, Joint exercises. Contact training cell for details.

Doctrine

Doctrine documents are maintained internally. Public summaries are available on request to authorised channels.
