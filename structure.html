Unit Structure

The Special Air Service is structured to provide maximum operational flexibility while maintaining tight command and control. Rest coming soon to the site!

Counter-Terrorism Team

A dedicated sub-unit maintains constant readiness for counter-terrorism operations. This team trains specifically in aircraft intervention, building entry, and hostage rescue, maintaining a higher state of readiness than the remainder of the unit. While drawn from the Squadrons, CT team members undergo additional selection and training in close quarter battle and precision shooting.

Medical Team

A dedicated sub-unit maintains constant readiness for medical procedures, when it's either in-field or in the infirmary. This team specializes in everything medical.
