Training Pipeline

The path to joining the Special Operations Group is demanding by design. Our training pipeline ensures that only those with the requisite physical stamina, mental resilience, and moral character earn the right to wear the beige beret.

Phase 1

Application

You apply in the discord.

Phase 2

Basic Training

Successful candidates enter a course covering basic tactics, weapons handling, communications, and medical training. This phase transitions the candidate from an individual to a team member, emphasising small unit tactics, fire and manoeuvre, and the basics of close quarter battle.
