Our Values

These principles guide our conduct on and off operations. They are non-negotiable and form the foundation of our operational effectiveness.

  • Discipline Unwavering self-control and adherence to standards regardless of circumstances. We maintain our equipment, physical fitness, and skills to the highest standard without external supervision. Discipline ensures we function effectively when exhausted, stressed, or isolated.
  • The rules There are a lot of rules, but the msot important thing is just to stay in role, follow chain of command, be a team, respect realism, and complete objectives.
